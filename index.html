The Commonplace Architecture

Most note-taking apps turn you into a filing cabinet. Commonplace is structured as a 4-layer cascade where every note, tool, and cadence serves actionable thought.

Layer 01
Mission
To extend and sharpen thinking—holding decisions made, ideas synthesized, and context needed again. Not storing everything ever read.
Layer 02
Values
Clarity, Intentionality, Depth, Serendipity, Leanness, Continuity, and Action. If you can't find or act on it effortlessly, the system has failed.
Layer 03
Principles
Write thoughts not facts. Prefer links over copies. Filter aggressively at capture. Todoist tracks doing; Obsidian tracks thinking.
Layer 04
Protocols
Repeatable operational step-by-steps: Daily Standups, Tool-Tier Ladders (cheapest AI tool first), and AuDHD reflective journaling.

Sniper, Not Sprayer Execution
